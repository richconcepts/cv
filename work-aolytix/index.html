Project Background

In a world of complex cloud infrastructure, Aolytix provides unparalleled visibility and understanding of resources. By visualizing the relationships between services, Aolytix empowers teams to easily identify potential security risks and inefficiencies, thus reducing costs and keeping systems secure and compliant.

The Problem

Modern cloud infrastructures are incredibly intricate, often spanning multiple providers and numerous services like Kubernetes. This complexity makes it challenging for teams to understand how their resources are connected and how they communicate. Itâ€™s like navigating a maze without a map â€“ you canâ€™t see the whole picture, and getting lost or encountering unexpected obstacles is very easy. This lack of visibility often leads to misconfigurations which is a leading cause of outages, security breaches, and lost business. Cloud teams need a way to cut through the noise.

The Solution

Aolytix is a powerful tool that provides unmatched insights into cloud infrastructures by taking data from various sources including AWS and Kubernetes clusters and displaying it in a clear visual graph. With Aolytix you gain an overall perspective of resources across your cloud environment as well as the exact connections and communication pathways between them.

By visualizing how the different services like pods, nodes and EC2 instances relate to one another you can quickly identify any gaps or vulnerabilities within your systems. Aolytix's Service Map illustrates how traffic flows through your application across AWS and Kubernetes. The visualizations also provides vital information regarding the ports and protocols that are used, highlighting potential misconfigurations by using a clear color-coded alert system.

Additionally, Aolytix helps identify network risks by showing you the configurations and connections between services. These visualizations help you spot areas that may be vulnerable to attacks. For example, identifying policies that allow dangerous outbound traffic is easier to spot with the way Aolytix visualizes connections.
